Tuesday — Vegetable-Stuffed Bell Peppers

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Method: Oven

vegetarianstuffed peppershealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 bell peppers (any color)
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup spinach (chopped)
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds.
  3. In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa, diced tomatoes, spinach, onion, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Stuff the mixture into the bell peppers and place upright in a baking dish.
  5. Top with cheese if desired and bake for 25-30 minutes.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure filling is soft and mix well to avoid choking hazards.
  • Cut peppers into small pieces after cooking for easier eating.
