Tuesday — Pressure Cooker Vegetable Lentil Stew

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure cooker

vegetarianlentilspressure cooker

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups organic lentils
  • 1 large organic carrot, diced
  • 1 large organic onion, diced
  • 2 cups organic kale, chopped
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pressure cooker, combine lentils, carrot, onion, kale, and spices.
  2. Pour in vegetable broth and seal lid.
  3. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes and release pressure manually.

Toddler Notes

  • Mash lentils for easier eating.
  • Ensure all vegetables are soft.
