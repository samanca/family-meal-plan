Monday — Quinoa Vegetable Bake

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Method: Oven

vegetarianquinoaoven

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups organic quinoa
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup organic bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 cup organic zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup organic spinach, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse quinoa and combine with vegetable broth in a baking dish.
  3. Add bell peppers, zucchini, spinach, and seasonings.
  4. Stir in olive oil and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork before serving.

Toddler Notes

  • Quinoa should be soft; mash lightly if needed.
  • Chop vegetables into small pieces.
