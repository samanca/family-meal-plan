Wednesday — Oven-Baked Salmon with Garlic Mashed Cauliflower and Steamed Green Beans

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenfishno porkno fried foodvegetablesmeat limit

Photo prompt: Oven-baked salmon fillets with garlic mashed cauliflower and vibrant green beans on a plate

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 organic salmon fillets (skin on)
  • 3 heads organic cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 4 cloves organic garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp unsalted organic butter
  • 500g organic green beans, trimmed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment; drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake salmon for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Steam green beans until tender crisp, about 5 minutes.
  5. Boil cauliflower florets until soft, about 10 minutes.
  6. Drain cauliflower and mash with garlic, butter, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  7. Serve salmon alongside mashed cauliflower and green beans with lemon wedges.

Toddler Notes

  • Flake salmon to remove bones and cut into small pieces.
  • Mash green beans slightly if needed for easier chewing.
