Sunday — Lemon Garlic Salmon, Roasted Potatoes & Broccoli

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenseafoodtoddler-friendlyunder 40 minutes

Ingredients for 6

  • 2.5 lb organic salmon fillets
  • 2 lb organic baby potatoes
  • 2 heads organic broccoli
  • 2 organic lemons
  • 4 cloves organic garlic
  • Organic olive oil
  • Salt-free or very light seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Halve potatoes and roast with olive oil for 15 minutes.
  3. Add broccoli and salmon to the sheet pan.
  4. Season with lemon, garlic, and olive oil.
  5. Roast 12–15 minutes, until salmon flakes easily.

Toddler Notes

  • Flake salmon carefully and check for bones.
  • Cut potatoes and broccoli into small soft pieces.
  • Keep seasoning mild.
