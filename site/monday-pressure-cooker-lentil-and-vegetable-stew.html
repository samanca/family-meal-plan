Monday — Pressure Cooker Lentil and Vegetable Stew

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

pressure cookervegetarianveganno meatno porkno fried food

Photo prompt: A warm bowl of lentil and vegetable stew garnished with fresh parsley

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups organic brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium organic onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves organic garlic, minced
  • 2 organic carrots, diced
  • 2 organic celery stalks, diced
  • 1 medium organic zucchini, chopped
  • 1 can (400g) organic diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups organic vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Set pressure cooker to sauté, add olive oil, onion, and garlic, cook until translucent.
  2. Add carrots, celery, zucchini, and sauté for 3 minutes.
  3. Add lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Seal pressure cooker and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow natural release for 10 minutes, then release remaining pressure.
  6. Stir and adjust seasoning if needed.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure lentils and vegetables are soft and cut into small pieces.
  • Serve lukewarm to avoid burns.
