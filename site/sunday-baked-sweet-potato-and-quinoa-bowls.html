Sunday — Baked Sweet Potato & Quinoa Bowls

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

vegangluten-freequick

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 medium sweet potatoes
  • 3 cups cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup black beans, canned, rinsed
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork and bake for 25-30 minutes until soft.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare quinoa as per instructions.
  4. In a bowl, combine black beans, diced bell pepper, lime juice, and cilantro.
  5. Once sweet potatoes are ready, cut in half, scoop some flesh if needed, and fill with quinoa mixture, topping with avocado slices.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure sweet potato is well-cooked and mashable.
  • Avoid skin; cut into small, manageable pieces for toddlers.
