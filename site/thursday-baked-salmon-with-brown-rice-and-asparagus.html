Thursday — Baked Salmon with Brown Rice & Asparagus

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

poultryquickhealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season salmon with olive oil, salt, and pepper; place on a baking sheet.
  3. Arrange asparagus around salmon and bake for 15-20 minutes until salmon is cooked.
  4. Prepare brown rice according to package instructions.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure salmon is flaked and devoid of bones.
  • Cut asparagus into smaller pieces.
