Thursday — Pressure Cooker Chickpea Curry

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure cooker

vegetarianchickpeaspressure cooker

Ingredients for 6

  • 3 cups organic chickpeas (cooked or canned)
  • 1 cup organic diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup organic coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups organic spinach

Instructions

  1. Combine chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and spices in the pressure cooker.
  2. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes, then release pressure.
  3. Stir in spinach before serving.

Toddler Notes

  • Make sure chickpeas are well-cooked.
  • Cut spinach into small pieces.
