Tuesday — Veggie Lentil Coconut Curry

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure cooker

pressure cookervegetarianlentilstoddler-friendly

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups organic red lentils
  • 2 cans organic coconut milk
  • 1 large bag organic spinach
  • 2 organic carrots
  • 1 organic onion
  • 2 cloves organic garlic
  • Mild curry powder

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils.
  2. Add lentils, coconut milk, carrots, onion, garlic, mild curry powder, and water as needed to pressure cooker.
  3. Cook on high pressure for 10–12 minutes.
  4. Stir in spinach after cooking until wilted.
  5. Thin with water or broth if needed.

Toddler Notes

  • Keep curry very mild.
  • Lentils should be soft and mashable.
  • Serve with rice if desired.
