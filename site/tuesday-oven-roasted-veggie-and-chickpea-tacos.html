Tuesday — Oven-Roasted Veggie & Chickpea Tacos

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

veganquicktacos

Ingredients for 6

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 6 whole grain tortillas
  • Avocado and salsa for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss chickpeas, bell peppers, and onion with olive oil and cumin. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 25 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas, fill with roasted mixture, and top with avocado and salsa.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure veggies are cooked soft and chickpeas are mashed if needed.
  • Serve filling in soft tortillas.
