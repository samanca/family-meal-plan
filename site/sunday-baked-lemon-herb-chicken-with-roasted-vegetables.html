Sunday — Baked Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenchickenmeatvegetablesno porkno fried food

Photo prompt: A colorful plate of baked lemon herb chicken with roasted carrots, broccoli, and sweet potatoes on a white serving dish

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 organic chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 500g organic baby carrots
  • 300g organic broccoli florets
  • 2 medium organic sweet potatoes, cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken breasts on a baking tray, drizzle with the herb mixture, and top with lemon slices.
  4. Arrange carrots, broccoli, and sweet potatoes around the chicken, tossing vegetables with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.

Toddler Notes

  • Cut chicken and vegetables into small, soft pieces appropriate for the toddler.
  • Avoid adding extra salt to toddler’s portions.
