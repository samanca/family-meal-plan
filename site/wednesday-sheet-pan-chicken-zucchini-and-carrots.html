Wednesday — Sheet Pan Chicken, Zucchini & Carrots

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenchickensheet panunder 40 minutes

Ingredients for 6

  • 2.5 lb organic chicken breast
  • 3 organic zucchini
  • 4 organic carrots
  • Organic olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Slice zucchini and carrots.
  3. Cut chicken into even pieces.
  4. Toss everything with olive oil and mild seasoning.
  5. Roast 25–30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.

Toddler Notes

  • Cut chicken into small pieces.
  • Make sure carrots are soft.
  • Serve with a moist side if chicken seems dry.
