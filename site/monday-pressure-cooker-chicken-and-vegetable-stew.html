Monday — Pressure Cooker Chicken & Vegetable Stew

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

poultryquickpressure cooker

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts, diced
  • 4 cups organic vegetable broth
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 2 zucchinis, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme

Instructions

  1. In pressure cooker, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add diced chicken, carrots, zucchini, broth, and thyme. Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  3. Release pressure, stir, and serve warm.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure chicken is well-cooked and shredded for toddlers.
  • Cut vegetables into small pieces.
