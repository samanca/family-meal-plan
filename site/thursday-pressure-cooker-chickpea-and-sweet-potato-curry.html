Thursday — Pressure Cooker Chickpea and Sweet Potato Curry

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

pressure cookervegetarianveganno meatno porkno fried food

Photo prompt: A bowl of chickpea and sweet potato curry garnished with fresh cilantro and served with brown rice

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups organic dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 2 medium organic sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium organic onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves organic garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh organic ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400ml) organic light coconut milk
  • 1 can (400g) organic diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp organic curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 4 cups organic vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups cooked organic brown rice (to serve)

Instructions

  1. Set pressure cooker to sauté, heat olive oil and cook onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  2. Add curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add chickpeas, sweet potatoes, diced tomatoes, coconut milk, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper.
  4. Seal the pressure cooker and cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.
  5. Allow natural release for 10 minutes, then release remaining pressure.
  6. Serve curry hot over cooked brown rice.

Toddler Notes

  • Cut sweet potatoes into small soft pieces.
  • Serve rice and curry cool enough and in small spoonfuls for toddler.
