Tuesday — Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chickpeas and Avocado

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenvegetarianveganno meatno porkno fried food

Photo prompt: A bowl of quinoa salad topped with roasted chickpeas, sliced avocado, cherry tomatoes, and fresh parsley

Ingredients for 6

  • 1.5 cups organic quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 can (400g) organic chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large organic avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup organic cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small organic red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh organic parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss chickpeas with olive oil, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper; spread on baking sheet.
  3. Roast chickpeas for 20 minutes, shaking halfway through.
  4. Cook quinoa according to package instructions, let cool slightly.
  5. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, roasted chickpeas, tomatoes, red onion, and parsley.
  6. Add lemon juice and extra olive oil if desired, toss gently.
  7. Top with avocado slices before serving.

Toddler Notes

  • Mash avocado slightly for easier eating.
  • Cut cherry tomatoes into small pieces to avoid choking hazards.
