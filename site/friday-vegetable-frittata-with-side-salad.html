Friday — Vegetable Frittata with Side Salad

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenvegetarianno meatno porkno fried food

Photo prompt: A golden vegetable frittata served with a fresh mixed green salad in a bowl

Ingredients for 6

  • 12 organic eggs
  • 1 cup organic spinach, chopped
  • 1 medium organic bell pepper, diced
  • 1 medium organic zucchini, diced
  • 1 small organic onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup organic cheddar cheese, shredded (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed greens (organic) for salad
  • Olive oil and lemon juice for dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion, bell pepper, zucchini, and spinach until tender.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs with salt and pepper; stir in cooked vegetables and cheese if using.
  4. Pour egg mixture into skillet over sautéed vegetables and cook on stove for 3-4 minutes until edges set.
  5. Transfer skillet to oven and bake for 12-15 minutes until frittata is fully set and golden.
  6. Serve warm with a side mixed green salad dressed with olive oil and lemon juice.

Toddler Notes

  • Cut frittata into small bite-sized pieces.
  • Use cheese according to toddler’s tolerance to dairy.
