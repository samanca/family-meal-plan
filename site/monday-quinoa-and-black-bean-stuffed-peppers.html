Monday — Quinoa & Black Bean Stuffed Peppers

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

quinoavegetarianstuffed peppers

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 large organic bell peppers
  • 1 cup cooked organic quinoa
  • 1 can (15 oz) organic black beans (rinsed)
  • 1 cup organic corn (frozen or fresh)
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded organic cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice tops off peppers and remove seeds.
  3. Mix quinoa, black beans, corn, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Stuff peppers with the mixture and place in a baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top if using, then cover and bake for 20 minutes.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure quinoa mixture is mashed slightly for easier eating.
  • Cut peppers into halves or quarters for safe serving.
