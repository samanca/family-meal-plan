Friday — Chickpea & Veggie Sheet Pan Bowls

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenvegetarianchickpeasunder 40 minutes

Ingredients for 6

  • 3 cans organic chickpeas
  • 2 organic zucchini
  • 2 organic bell peppers
  • 1 organic onion
  • Organic olive oil
  • Cumin
  • Leftover rice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Drain and rinse chickpeas.
  3. Chop zucchini, bell peppers, and onion.
  4. Roast chickpeas and vegetables with olive oil and mild cumin for 25 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice.

Toddler Notes

  • Mash chickpeas lightly to reduce choking risk.
  • Cut vegetables small.
  • Keep seasoning mild.
