Friday — Brown Rice and Grilled Vegetable Bowl

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

veganrice bowlvegetables

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 zucchini (sliced)
  • 2 bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse brown rice and cook in water according to package instructions.
  3. Toss sliced vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve vegetables over rice.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure rice is soft and well-cooked.
  • Cut roasted vegetables into small, bite-sized pieces.
