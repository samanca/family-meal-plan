Friday — Vegetable & Bean Stir-Fry with Quinoa

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

veganquickstir-fry

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. In pressure cooker, sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and kidney beans; stir-fry for 10 minutes, adding soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Serve over quinoa.

Toddler Notes

  • Ensure vegetables are soft and cut into small manageable pieces.
  • Quinoa should be fluffy and easy to chew.
