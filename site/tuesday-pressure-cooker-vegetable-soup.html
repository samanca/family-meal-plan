Tuesday — Pressure Cooker Vegetable Soup

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

soupveganlentilsquick

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 cups organic vegetable broth
  • 2 cups organic diced tomatoes (canned)
  • 2 cups mixed organic vegetables (carrots, celery, peas)
  • 1 cup organic lentils
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to the pressure cooker.
  2. Seal and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, then release pressure.
  3. Serve warm.

Toddler Notes

  • Blend or mash soup before serving to ensure a smooth texture.
  • Avoid whole lentils for easier consumption.
