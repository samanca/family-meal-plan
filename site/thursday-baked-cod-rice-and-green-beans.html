Thursday — Baked Cod, Rice & Green Beans

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Oven

ovenseafoodricetoddler-friendly

Ingredients for 6

  • 2.5 lb wild or responsibly sourced cod
  • 1 lb organic green beans
  • 2 cups organic brown rice
  • 1 organic lemon
  • Organic olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  3. Place cod and green beans on sheet pan.
  4. Season with lemon and olive oil.
  5. Bake cod 12–15 minutes until flaky.

Toddler Notes

  • Flake cod carefully and check for bones.
  • Cut green beans into small pieces.
  • Serve rice moist if needed.
