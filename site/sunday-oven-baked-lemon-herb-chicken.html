Sunday — Oven-Baked Lemon Herb Chicken

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

chickenovenhealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 organic chicken breasts
  • 3 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 2 cups organic broccoli florets
  • 2 cups organic baby carrots

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place chicken in a baking dish.
  3. Squeeze juice of 2 lemons over chicken.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  5. Arrange broccoli and carrots around chicken.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.

Toddler Notes

  • Shred chicken for easier chewing.
  • Ensure broccoli is soft and cut into small florets.
