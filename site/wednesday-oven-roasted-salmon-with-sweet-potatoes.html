Wednesday — Oven-Roasted Salmon with Sweet Potatoes

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

salmonovenhealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 6 organic salmon fillets
  • 3 organic sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut sweet potatoes into wedges and toss with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place on a baking sheet and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Add salmon to the same sheet and roast for an additional 12-15 minutes.

Toddler Notes

  • Flake salmon to avoid choking hazards.
  • Ensure sweet potato is soft and cut into manageable pieces.
