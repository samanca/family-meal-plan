Sunday — Baked Lemon Herb Chicken & Veggies

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Method: Oven

chickenbakedvegetableshealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 12 oz organic chicken breast
  • 3 cups mixed organic vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 tsp dried herbs (thyme, rosemary)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, zest, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss chicken and vegetables in the mixture.
  4. Spread on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes.

Toddler Notes

  • Cut chicken into small, bite-size pieces to reduce choking risk.
  • Ensure vegetables are soft and cut into small shapes.
