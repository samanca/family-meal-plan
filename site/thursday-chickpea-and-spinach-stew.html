Thursday — Chickpea and Spinach Stew

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Method: Pressure Cooker

veganstewhealthy

Ingredients for 6

  • 2 cups canned chickpeas (rinsed)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In the pressure cooker, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chickpeas, broth, spinach, cumin, and salt.
  3. Seal and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes with a natural release.

Toddler Notes

  • Mash or blend stew to a smoother texture for toddlers.
  • Ensure no large chickpeas or pieces that could cause choking.
